PDF tools for Windows

Download BenchPDF

For Word and Excel, the desktop app converts through the copy of Office already on your PC, so the output is what Word and Excel themselves would produce. It also edits PDF text in place.

Download BenchPDF 1.0.0 for Windows ↘ Free during early access.

Windows 10 and 11. Office conversions need your own copy of Office.

What the desktop app adds

  • Word, Excel and PowerPoint conversions, run through your own installed Office rather than a separate engine imitating it.
  • PDF to PowerPoint that keeps paragraphs whole and rebuilds ruled tables as real PowerPoint tables.
  • PDF text editing in place, where the pages you did not touch are written back byte for byte identical.
  • Web page to PDF, rendered by a browser engine running on your own machine.
  • Fonts matched against the ones actually installed on your PC, which a browser cannot read.

What it does not do

It is Windows only, so there is no Mac or Linux build. It has no OCR, so a scanned PDF has no text for it to recover. The Office conversions need Office installed, and are greyed out with an explanation if it is not found. Everything else runs without it.

If you would rather not install anything

PDF to Images, PDF to Text and Images to PDF run in your browser on the home page, on any operating system, with nothing to install.

PowerPoint to PDF converter

Export slides to PDF using your own copy of PowerPoint, settled into their final frame.

Download BenchPDF for Windows Windows 10/11 · v1.0.0 · free, no account

BenchPDF drives your own installed copy of Microsoft PowerPoint to export the PDF, rather than reconstructing the deck from its file format separately. Fonts, embedded video posters, and each slide's final animation frame come out the way PowerPoint itself renders them, because it's PowerPoint doing the rendering.

Presentations are also frequently the file people are most careful about — pitch decks, unreleased product plans, client work — and an online converter asks you to upload exactly that to a server you don't control before you can even see the PDF. BenchPDF's conversion never leaves your PC.

There's no file size ceiling tied to someone else's free tier, no queue, and no account. If PowerPoint isn't installed, the option is greyed out with an honest note instead of quietly failing partway through.

Images to PDF converter

Combine several photos or scans into one PDF, in the order you drop them.

Download BenchPDF for Windows Windows 10/11 · v1.0.0 · free, no account

Drop several image files — including HEIC photos straight from an iPhone — onto BenchPDF and they merge, in the order you dropped them, into a single multi-page PDF sized to each image. There's no separate “merge” step and no re-upload between files.

The common case is scanning something with a phone camera: a receipt, a form, a handwritten note, an ID document, several pages of a contract laid out on a desk — and needing one PDF out the other end to attach to an email or print. That last example is worth naming directly: photographs of ID documents, boarding passes, and insurance cards are some of the most sensitive images people convert, and among the least scrutinised before being handed to a random “image to PDF” website.

BenchPDF does the merge locally, so those photos never leave the device they were taken on, and there's no size limit forcing a lower-quality re-compression before the PDF comes back.
